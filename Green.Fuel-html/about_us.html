About Us

WHO ARE WE


Green Fuel is a social enterprise dedicated to providing sustainable access to clean cooking energy through innovative solutions. We operate "Green Fuel fill in and cook" that produces and sells bio cooking gas for households and businesses that generate lower quantities of biodegradable waste that can't produce the energy needed for daily consumption. We also operate "Green Fuel Iwawe", installing bio-generating energy system technology for farmers and institutions capable of maintaining the systems and producing energy for their consumption needs.
